Privacy Policy for ENOPS

Last Updated:

Welcome to ENOPS ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile and web applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the ENOPS application.

1. Information We Collect

ENOPS is designed to streamline hospital operations, including inventory management, equipment maintenance, and patient handling. To provide these services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When hospital staff create an account, we collect email addresses, full names, phone numbers, employee numbers, and job roles.
  • Patient Data: Authorized staff may input patient data into the app, including names, dates of birth, genders, addresses, contact numbers, and Hospital Record Numbers (HRNs).
  • Device and Camera Information: With your permission, our app accesses your device's camera and photo library to allow you to upload profile avatars, patient photos, and images of inventory or equipment.
  • Operational Data: We collect data related to hospital operations, such as inventory stock levels, billing invoices, and equipment maintenance logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the ENOPS platform for your hospital or clinic.
  • To facilitate secure authentication and account management.
  • To manage hospital operations, track inventory and assets, and handle billing and patient directories.
  • To improve our application, develop new features, and provide customer support.

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data. Your information, including images and operational data, is securely transmitted to and stored on our backend servers (powered by Supabase). We utilize Row Level Security (RLS) and authentication tokens to ensure that data is only accessible to authorized personnel within your specific hospital or clinic.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information or patient data to third parties. We may share information only in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (like our secure cloud hosting provider, Supabase) who assist us in operating our application.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you. Hospital administrators have the ability to manage staff accounts and patient records directly within the ENOPS app.

6. Deleting Your User Information

To request the deletion of your user information, please contact us at pkinanang@gmail.com. We will process your request in accordance with applicable laws.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date on this page and, where appropriate, providing a notice within the app.
